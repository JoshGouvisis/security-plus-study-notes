
# 5.2 Risk Management Processes
* **Risk Identification:** Documenting potential threats to organizational assets.
* **Assessments:**
    * **Qualitative:** Risk ranking (High/Medium/Low) based on impact and likelihood.
    * **Quantitative:** Mathematical calculation (ALE = SLE x ARO).
* **Risk Treatment:** Accept, Avoid, Mitigate, or Transfer (Insurance).
* **BIA (Business Impact Analysis):** Determining RTO (Recovery Time Objective) and RPO (Recovery Point Objective).
