## 🎯 Domain Overview
This domain focuses on the governance, risk management, and compliance (GRC) aspects of cybersecurity. It covers how organizations align security with business goals, manage third-party risks, and adhere to legal and regulatory frameworks.

## 📝 Objective Progress
- [ ] 5.1 Compare and contrast elements of effective security governance.
- [ ] 5.2 Explain elements of the risk management process.
- [ ] 5.3 Explain the processes associated with third-party risk assessment and management.
- [ ] 5.4 Summarize types and purposes of compliance frameworks and legal considerations.
- [ ] 5.5 Explain the importance of audits and assessments in an organization’s security posture.
- [ ] 5.6 Explain the structures and types of security policies and guidelines.
